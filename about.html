Our Story & Mission

Decision infrastructure, not demos.

SageLogic AI Solutions is a boutique AI consulting firm focused on applied decision-support systems for professional services organizations.

Our Mission

Jack Larriviere

Founder and Principle Consultant

SageLogic helps organizations apply artificial intelligence responsibly to improve decision-making and operational performance. We design AI systems that support real workflows, reduce errors, and preserve institutional knowledge — while keeping people accountable for outcomes.

We do not sell hype. We deliver AI that works in practice.

Our Vision

AI earns trust when it is designed with discipline, transparency, and clear limits. Our vision is a future where organizations adopt AI confidently because its role is well-defined, its outputs are reliable, and responsibility is always clear.

SageLogic exists for teams that need AI to be dependable, defensible, and aligned with real-world accountability.

What We Stand For

Accountability First

AI supports people — it does not replace responsibility. Every system we build keeps human ownership and review at the center.

Accuracy and Reliability

Correct, consistent outputs matter more than novelty. Our systems escalate or pause rather than guess.

Responsible Design

Clear scope, defined boundaries, and appropriate safeguards are essential to trustworthy AI.

Business Impact That Matters

We focus on outcomes that reduce friction and risk: fewer errors, smoother operations, better use of time.

Respect for Professional Judgment

In regulated and high-stakes environments, expertise matters. Our AI assists qualified professionals — it does not bypass them.

Long-Term Trust

We value durable partnerships built on candid communication, thoughtful risk management, and disciplined delivery.

Our Approach

We design and deploy AI as decision infrastructure — integrated into your operations, aligned with your standards, and governed from day one. Our approach emphasizes clarity, control, and measurable improvement over experimentation.

No black boxes. No autonomous decisions. No unsupervised outputs.

What We Commit To

  • Delivering AI systems that fit your real workflows — not a generic template applied to your data
  • Improved consistency without sacrificing human accountability
  • Systems designed with safety and governance built in from day one
  • Transparent communication on scope, risk, and feasibility before any build begins
  • Trust earned through reliability, not promises
